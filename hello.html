Dear ITS Students, Asia Technological University Network (ATU-Net) & Phenikaa University (PU) Vietnam will be hosting the ATU-Net Student Leader Forum 2024 (ATU-Net SeLF 2024) from 11th to 13th September 2024 in Hanoi, Vietnam. Featuring the theme “Fostering Diversity, Equity and Inclusion (D.E.I.) through Student Leaders”, the ATU-Net SeLF 2024 will equip participants with keynote sessions, group discussions and presentations on the importance of D.E.I. as well as exposing students to the Vietnamese culture and surroundings. Details of the programme are as follows: Dates: 11th to 13th September 2024 Venue: Phenikaa University, Hanoi, Vietnam Fee: Free for ITS Students since ITS students are part of ATU Net members.

What will be included: i. On-campus dormitory accommodation from 10th to 14th September 2024 ii. Local transportation iii. Meals during the programme iv. Participants will be awarded with e-certificates at the end of the programme. The selected students must responsible with roundtrip flight ticket, visa fees, insurance and other personal expenses Every institution is eligible for two seats of waived participation fee which is based on the first come first serve basis, limited to up to 40 participants.
